About The Player

Amir Rrahmani is a Kosovan professional footballer who plays as a center-back for the Italian Serie A club Napoli and the Kosovo national team. Born on February 24, 1994, in Kosovo, Rrahmani began his professional career at the age of 18 with the Kosovan club KF Liria. He later played for other Kosovan clubs such as KF Besa, KF Prishtina, and KF Gjilani, before moving to Italy in 2018 to join Hellas Verona. After impressing at Verona, Rrahmani signed for Napoli in January 2021. He is known for his physicality, aerial ability, and strong defensive skills, making him a valuable asset for both club and country. Rrahmani has represented the Kosovo national team since 2017, helping them secure historic wins against countries like the Czech Republic and Bulgaria. At 29 years old, Rrahmani is considered one of the best defenders in Kosovo and has a bright future ahead of him in Italian football.
